# 06 — County Landing Pages: Messaging

No messaging surface. County landing pages are read-only public web pages —
they send no SMS, no email, and trigger no notification or queue work.

Included for parity with sibling spec folders ([[feedback-doc-style]]). If a
future iteration adds "notify me when jobs open in this county" (a county job
alert), that becomes a real messaging surface and this file is where its SMS /
email templates, quiet-hours behaviour, and idempotency contract are specified —
out of scope for MVP (see [01-overview](01-overview.md) → Out of scope).
